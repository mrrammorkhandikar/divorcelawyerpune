Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How much does a consultation cost?

We offer a free initial consultation to understand your case and provide you with the best legal advice. This consultation helps us assess your situation and explain your legal options.

How long does a divorce case take?

The duration of a divorce case depends on various factors including the complexity of the case, whether it's contested or uncontested, and court schedules. We work efficiently to resolve cases as quickly as possible.

Do you handle cases outside Pune?

Yes, we handle cases throughout Maharashtra and can provide legal assistance for cases in other states as well. We also offer online consultations for clients who cannot visit our office.

What documents do I need for consultation?

For the initial consultation, you don't need to bring any documents. However, if you have any relevant documents like marriage certificate, property papers, or court notices, you can bring them for a more detailed review.
